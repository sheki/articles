California is trying to be the leader in climate policy

California is trying to set an example by adopting really agressive cap and trade goals. If this legislation SB755 passes it will create a free market for carbon trading and abolish carbon offsets.

There are a few interesting things about this legislation one being a Border adjustment tax to be levied on imports from other states where carbon emission tax / cost is cheaper. If this is executed well, this might lead other states to adopt similar strickter measueres. The downside is a lot of industry can move out of california to places where producing carbon is cheaper.

The legislation has a huge potential of producing lots of income for the state. This income is being allocated to per-capita dividends, it is a nice effort at redistribution as most of the money will go to the poorer people.
