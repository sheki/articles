Trade is not a zero sum game

An economist studied the effect of railway in India after the 1850s. According to the study the annual income in India increased by 16% thanks to the railway. This is apparently proof for trade not being a zero sum game.

A simpler example is a community when it comes in contact with others, can stop doing what they are really bad at and focus on the good things. This usuall leads to higher output.
