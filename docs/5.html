Cities and chain stores

Hayes Valley in SF banned chain stores from opening in the neighborhood. Now it is full of boutique stores that are not useful to the locals.

French have had rules against chain stores for a long time. There is probably an underlying problem of smaller stores not being able to compete and hence cities need to have laws against chain stores.
